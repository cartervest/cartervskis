Who I Am

I’m Carter, a BYU student studying Economics with a Business minor. I’m fascinated by the way numbers, strategy, and technology shape the world around us, and I love applying those tools to real problems—whether in real estate, startups, or potential future work in law and finance. I’m driven by curiosity and a desire to build things that make life more efficient, meaningful, or enjoyable.

Outside of academics, I enjoy running, working on creative side projects, and learning about investing and personal finance. I’m happiest when I’m outdoors or on the water with friends and family, and I strive to keep a balance between work, learning, and experiences that help me grow.

This page was built completely from scratch using HTML and CSS as a way to practice and demonstrate core web development skills. I’m always looking for opportunities to grow, collaborate, and take on meaningful projects—so feel free to reach out if you’d like to connect.
